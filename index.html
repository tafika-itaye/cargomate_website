About Us

Who Is Cargomate?

Cargomate Limited is a Malawi-based logistics and customs clearing company headquartered in Lilongwe. We specialise in freight forwarding, customs compliance, and cross-border trade facilitation, serving importers, exporters, and businesses of all sizes across the country and the region.

Our operational footprint spans multiple border posts and inland locations, supported by an experienced team of clearing agents, logistics coordinators, and client service professionals committed to efficiency and transparency.
