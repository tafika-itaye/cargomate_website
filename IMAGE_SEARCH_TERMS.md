## 🌍 Cultural Notes

- All people images should reflect **Malawian / southern African** culture and professional appearance.
- Avoid generic Western stock photos.
- Border post and road images should ideally show **Malawi or SADC region** geography.
- Recommended free sources: **Unsplash**, **Pexels**, **Pixabay** (search terms above work well on all three).
