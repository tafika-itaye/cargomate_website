# About Cargomate Limited

**Cargomate Limited** is a Malawi-based logistics and customs clearing company headquartered in Lilongwe. We specialise in freight forwarding, customs compliance, and cross-border trade facilitation, serving importers, exporters, and businesses of all sizes across Malawi and the wider SADC region.

## Our Mission

To provide businesses with reliable, transparent, and technology-driven logistics solutions that reduce clearance times, ensure compliance, and deliver full cargo visibility from origin to destination.

## What We Do

- **Customs Clearing** — Expert documentation, tariff classification, and MRA compliance
- **Freight Forwarding** — Road, air, and sea freight across the SADC region
- **Warehousing & Handling** — Secure handling and inventory management
- **Client Portal** — Digital shipment tracking and document management
- **Compliance Advisory** — Import/export regulatory guidance

## Our Reach

Cargomate operates at multiple border posts including Mchinji, Mwanza, Dedza, and Songwe, as well as Lilongwe and Blantyre offices and Kamuzu International Airport.
